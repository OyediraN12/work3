Love Is In The Andalusian Air… Glamours Nobu Hotel Marbella Is More Enticing And Romantic Than Ever

The voguish and sexy Nobu Hotel Marbella has upped the ante as a mini-moon destination for newly-weds this year. Already renowned for hosting unforgettable stays with extraordinary dining, chic rooms and epic nightlife in an enviable location, in celebration of its first birthday Nobu Hotel Marbella has launched new imaginative and ‘Instagrammable’ experiences that can be enjoyed for a memorable mini-moon.

For wild romantics, a private helicopter can be booked to take in the unbelievable panoramic views of Marbella. This experience comes paired with a delicious tapas lunch in the neighbouring district of Ronda. This year is all about guests immersing themselves in the spirit of the hotel and the culture of the destination by being able to take experiences home with them. With this in mind, couples can master the art of sushi making with the head Nobu chef, learn the passion of flamenco dancing or have a private paddle boarding lesson on the glassy waters of the Med.

For couple’s who don’t want to leave the famously comfortable, oversized Nobu beds, the hotel offers in-room Nobu dining, so guests can enjoy world-class Japanese cuisine from the comfort of their suite. In the rooms, there are matching black kimonos with slippers and a cocktail is brought to the door via the roving mixology cart for the ultimate romantic Nobu night in.

Created with the sophisticated sybarite in mind, Nobu Hotel Marbella curates a unique guest experience that blends the best of Andalusian charm, hospitality and unbeatable weather; with the attention to detail, signature design and contemporary flair for which the Nobu brand is famous.

With a design following the same minimalistic and chic theme throughout, this stylish resort exudes an air of excitement, reflecting the globally-recognised ‘Nobu Experience’ with the vitality of Andalucía, continuing to allow an exciting and culturally immersive experience, even when not indulging in one of the unbelievable new experiences.

Rates start from £295 per night on a bed and breakfast basis. Further information is available at nobuhotelmarbella.com or SLH.com
