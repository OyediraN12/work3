Late September Sun

Gennadi Grand Resort & Spa

PACKAGE: Book 7 nights at Gennadi Grand Resort, Rhodes for £663pp. Price is based on 2 adults sharing a Double Garden View Room, on a bed and breakfast basis. Price includes return flights from London Gatwick based 28th September departure. www.britishairways.com

Set snugly between mountain and sea, family-friendly Gennadi Grand Resort lies within a small fishing village off the tourist trail. Leading the way for luxury, the resort is a place where design, beauty and architecture are seamlessly fused. Lovingly landscaped and thoughtfully conceived by eco-architects, the resort is a fresh breath of Aegean air offering sun, sea and sustainability. Most of the 288 rooms offer private pools along with slanted roofs designed to minimise sunlight reflection and optimise the microclimate conditions. Naturally uplifting and nourishing for mind, body and soul, the resort also boasts an impressive spa as well as twelve delectable bars and restaurants.

www.gennadigrandresort.com

Lindos Village Resort & Spa

PACKAGE: Book 7 nights at Lindos Village Resort & Spa, Rhodes for £695pp. Price is based on 2 adults sharing a Deluxe Garden View Room, on a bed and breakfast basis. Price includes return flights from London Gatwick based 21st September departure. www.britishairways.com

Sink into traditional Greek life where white washed walls artfully blend with a soothing palate of blues, the recently renovated five-star Lindos Village Resort & Spa invites adults to unwind on one of Rhodes’ most tranquil coastlines with a selection of inspiring itineraries.

Taking advantage of the resort’s peaceful location, a walk along the Philosopher’s Kleoboulos trail as the sun rises is not to be missed, and for those wanting to explore further can embark on a leisurely boat trip to the nearby island of Symi – famously known for its delectable shrimps. Back at the resort, guests can connect with nature with a horseback trek through the neighbouring valleys and coves or head onto the water with plentiful water sports to be enjoyed including sea fishing, windsurfing, canoeing, scuba diving and water skiing.

www.lindos-village.gr

Lindos Grand Resort & Spa

PACKAGE: Book 7 nights at Lindos Village Resort & Spa, Rhodes for £565pp. Price is based on 2 adults sharing a Deluxe Garden View Room, on a bed and breakfast basis. Price includes return flights from London Gatwick based 28th September departure. www.britishairways.com

The stylish, adults-only Lindos Grand Resort & Spa is the island’s hottest new chic and contemporary hotel, boasting breath-taking views across the sandy shores of Vlycha Bay. Effortlessly fusing tradition and the natural beauty of Rhodes with sheer sophistication, here rooms and suites proudly savour the bay’s spectacular views with wrap-around infinity pools offering pure, unadulterated delight. As the sun sets, the Lounge Piano Bar is perfect for sipping cool cocktails before discovering the resort’s gastronomical delights – where global flavours and inventive dishes fuse the flavours of Greece, the Mediterranean and the Far East. 

www.lindosgrand.com
